About Us

Surya Defence Academy, located in Pathankot, is a leading institute dedicated to grooming aspirants for various defense and paramilitary recruitment exams. The academy provides expert coaching for Army Agniveer, SSC GD, CRPF, CISF, BSF, ITBP, and state police forces, ensuring students receive top-tier training to succeed in their respective careers. With a commitment to affordable education, the academy offers special concessions for children of Veer Naries, ex-servicemen, and those in need of financial support. Focused on holistic development, Surya Defence Academy integrates both written coaching and rigorous physical training, equipping students with the skills and discipline required to excel in defense services. For detailed information, visit our official website.
